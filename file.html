AYFT

What does AYFT stand for?
11 4
+7

Are you free today?

Co-workers, friends, and family members who need a moment of your time may use AYFT to mean "are you free today?" For example, a co-worker who wants to meet in person may send a message that says "AYFT to meet? How does 2:30 in conference room A sound?"

However, beware! People also often ask AYFT before asking for a favor, such as "AYFT? ... cool, can you pick me up from the airport?" So maybe think twice before answering in the affirmative to AYFT ...

Example

AYFT?
Yeah, what do you wanna do?
Could you watch Buster for me? I'm gonna go out.
